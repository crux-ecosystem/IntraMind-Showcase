## The Daily Pain

A junior associate spends 2 hours finding the right BNS section for a client brief. A senior partner can't verify a Supreme Court ratio during a court hearing. An amendment from last session's legislation hasn't reached anyone's desk. An appeal deadline slips because limitation was calculated from the wrong date.

**These aren't edge cases. This is every law firm, every day.**

<br />

## What IntraMind Does

A single query interface sitting on a server inside your firm. The advocate types a question in plain language. IntraMind returns the exact answer — with section, sub-section, proviso, and citation — in seconds.

<br />

### Example Queries

> *"What are the bail provisions for a non-bailable offense under BNS where the accused is a minor?"*  
> → Returns: Exact BNS sections, BNSS procedural requirements, Juvenile Justice Act intersection, relevant Supreme Court guidelines. All cited.

> *"Limitation period for filing a civil suit for recovery of immovable property"*  
> → Returns: Article 65 of the Limitation Act, starting point rules, exceptions, Supreme Court interpretations. Cited to paragraph.

> *"What changed in Section 498A equivalents under BNS?"*  
> → Returns: Old IPC provision vs. new BNS section, with exact textual comparison and effective date.
