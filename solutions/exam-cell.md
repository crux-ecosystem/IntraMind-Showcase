---

## What It Does

Exam cell staff query examination regulations, scheduling rules, and result processing procedures in plain language. IntraMind returns the exact regulation, procedure, or precedent — cited from the institution's exam cell documents.

<br />

### Use Cases

- **Regulation lookup** — "What is the re-evaluation procedure and timeline?" → Exact regulation with section.
- **Scheduling assistance** — "Clash-free scheduling constraints for 3rd semester CS and IT exams" → Rules from uploaded SOPs.
- **Result processing** — "Grace mark policy for final-year students" → Policy document citation.

<br />

## Services

| Service | What It Solves |
|---------|---------------|
| **Exam Regulation Search** | University/institution exam rules searchable in plain language |
| **Scheduling Guidance** | Rules and constraints for exam scheduling from uploaded SOPs |
| **Result Processing Reference** | Grace marks, re-evaluation, supplementary exam policies |
| **Compliance Tracking** | University/affiliating body requirements with citations |
