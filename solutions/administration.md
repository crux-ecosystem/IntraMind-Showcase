---

## What It Does

Administrative staff ask questions about institutional policies, regulations, and procedures in plain language. IntraMind returns the relevant policy section, form, or procedural step — cited from the institution's own uploaded documents.

No more digging through 200-page regulation booklets. No more "ask the senior clerk."

<br />

### Use Cases

- **Policy lookup** — "What is the leave policy for contract faculty?" → Exact regulation with section number.
- **Procedure guidance** — "Steps for processing a student transfer request" → Step-by-step from the admin manual.
- **Regulatory compliance** — "UGC requirements for faculty-student ratio" → Cited provision with latest amendment.

<br />

## Services

| Service | What It Solves |
|---------|---------------|
| **Policy Search** | Natural language queries against institutional regulation documents |
| **Workflow Guidance** | Step-by-step procedures for administrative processes |
| **Compliance Reference** | UGC/AICTE/university regulation lookup with citations |
| **Staff Knowledge Base** | Internal memos, circulars, and precedent decisions searchable |
