# IntraMind — Enterprise

> We deploy AI inside institutions. Not to them.

---

## How We Work

IntraMind is not a download. It's not an app store product. It's not a SaaS subscription.

**IntraMind is deployed through direct engagement** — our team works with your institution from scoping to handover. We install, configure, load your domain content, train your staff, and ensure the system operates at production quality before we leave.

<br />

## Engagement Phases

| Phase | What Happens | Duration |
|-------|-------------|----------|
| **1. Discovery** | Requirements study. Content audit. Stakeholder interviews. We understand your institution's specific needs, document landscape, and security requirements. | 1–2 weeks |
| **2. Infrastructure** | Server deployment on-site. Platform installation. Network configuration (or confirmation that no network is needed). | 1–2 weeks |
| **3. Domain Build** | Domain-specific knowledge base creation. Content ingestion. Guardrail configuration. UI customization. Role and permission setup. | 8–12 weeks |
| **4. Pilot** | Controlled deployment with 2–3 departments or teams. Real usage. Feedback collection. Quality evaluation. | 4 weeks |
| **5. Full Deployment** | Institution-wide rollout. Staff training. Documentation handover. Performance baseline established. | 2–4 weeks |
| **6. Ongoing Support** | Content update assistance. Performance monitoring. Issue resolution. On-site or remote (your choice). | Ongoing |

**Typical engagement: 4–7 months from first meeting to full production.**

<br />

## Pricing Philosophy

We don't do per-seat licensing. We don't do annual subscriptions. We don't do usage-based billing.

| Model | What It Covers |
|-------|---------------|
| **Hardware** | One-time purchase. Server sits in your building. You own it. INR 3–8 lakhs depending on configuration. |
| **Deployment** | One-time engagement fee. Discovery through full deployment. Includes domain build, training, handover. |
| **Support** | Optional ongoing support contract. Priced annually. Scope defined with your IT team. |

**No cloud bills. No hidden fees. No "contact us for pricing" games.**

> Specific pricing provided after Discovery phase, based on institution size, edition complexity, and support requirements.

## Hardware

One server. Desktop-sized. Sits in a room you control.

| Spec | Minimum | Recommended |
|------|---------|-------------|
| CPU | 16 cores | 32 cores |
| RAM | 32 GB | 64 GB |
| Storage | 1 TB SSD | 2 TB NVMe SSD |
| GPU | 24 GB VRAM | 48 GB VRAM |
| Network | Institution LAN | Gigabit LAN |
| Internet | **Not required** | Not required |

We can recommend specific hardware configurations based on your institution's scale and budget. We can also procure and pre-configure the server before on-site deployment.
